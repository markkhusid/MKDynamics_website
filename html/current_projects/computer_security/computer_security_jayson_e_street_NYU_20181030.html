Notes from presentation

Jayson suggests starting in Blue Teaming first.

Red teams make money not by breaking in to systems, but by showing the client how to fix it.

Jayson started out in IT support for online bank in 2000, then went on to Network Security support.

Need to learn about firewalls, Intrusion Detection Systems, and Network Segmentation.

He started Red Teaming 8 years later, and got into social engineering.

He used fake badges and rubber ducky type USB hacking devices.

Never physically write down a forged email address. Have it on a tablet instead.

Forward the email multiple times makes it more believable.
